The 2025 AI Sales Stack:
Moving Beyond ChatGPT.

AI usage among sales reps rose 79% year-over-year. But the most effective teams aren't just copy-pasting prompts; they are building purpose-built ecosystems.

From General-Purpose to Purpose-Built

Only 19% of sales reps use AI features built directly into their tools; most still use generic chatbots. This workflow misses the critical CRM context needed for meaningful automation. By 2028, 60% of B2B seller work will be through conversational AI interfaces built into their tech stack.

The Rise of Signal-Based Selling

The 2025 stack isn't about more emails; it's about better signals. Trigger-based outreach timed to specific buyer events (like leadership changes or funding) generates 400% higher conversion rates than standard cold outreach.
